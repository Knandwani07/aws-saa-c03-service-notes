# AWS Health Dashboard

---

## What It Is

The AWS Health Dashboard provides real-time visibility into the operational status and health of AWS services, both globally and specifically for your AWS account. It helps you track service disruptions, planned maintenance, and resource-level issues that could impact your workloads.

Two main views:

1. **Service Health Dashboard** – Public, AWS-wide service status across regions  
2. **Your Account Health Dashboard** – Personalized view showing issues affecting your specific AWS resources  

---

## Key Features

• Real-time service status updates for outages, degradation, and maintenance  
• Personalized notifications for account-specific incidents  
• Event history for reviewing past service or maintenance issues  
• Integrates with CloudWatch and SNS for automated operational alerts  
• AWS Health API available for programmatic monitoring and automation  

---

## Use Cases

• Monitoring AWS service interruptions impacting production workloads  
• Tracking infrastructure health during operational or incident response  
• Setting up automated notifications via SNS or EventBridge  
• Reviewing historical events for compliance reviews or root cause analysis  

---

## Exam Tips

• Service Health Dashboard = public, no login required  
• Account Health Dashboard = requires login, shows resource-level issues  
• AWS Health API enables automation and integration into monitoring systems  
• Common exam context: troubleshooting, resilience design, or operational visibility  

---

## Quick Summary

AWS Health Dashboard gives you global and account-specific visibility into AWS service health, enabling proactive monitoring, incident awareness, and resilient architecture planning.
