# AWS Well-Architected Tool

---

## What It Is
AWS Well-Architected Tool is a **free**, **guided assessment service** that helps you review and improve your cloud workloads using the AWS Well-Architected Framework. It identifies architectural risks, provides actionable improvement plans, and enables consistent, repeatable reviews across teams.

---

## Key Features

• Guided workload reviews using AWS best practices  
• Identifies High-Risk Issues (HRIs) and Medium-Risk Issues (MRIs)  
• Stores historical reviews for comparison and tracking  
• Generates prioritized improvement plans  
• Supports custom lenses for specialized workloads  
• Accessible directly from the AWS Management Console  

---

## AWS Well-Architected Framework Pillars

The tool is built on AWS’s 6-pillar Well-Architected Framework:

• **Operational Excellence** – Operate, monitor, evolve  
• **Security** – Protect systems, data, and assets  
• **Reliability** – Maintain workload performance & recovery  
• **Performance Efficiency** – Use resources efficiently  
• **Cost Optimization** – Reduce unnecessary cost  
• **Sustainability** – Reduce environmental impact  

Each pillar contains design principles, best practices, and review questions.

---

## Workload Reviews

• Define and document workloads for assessment  
• Answer structured questions across all six pillars  
• Identify HRIs/MRIs for improvement  
• Add **custom lenses** for industry-specific or organizational best practices  
• Use reviews to ensure readiness before production deployment  

---

## Improvement Plans

• Automatically generated based on identified risks  
• Prioritized tasks to remediate architecture gaps  
• Linked to AWS docs and recommended solutions  
• Track progress of completed vs. pending improvements  
• Enables teams to collaborate on architectural remediation  

---

## Lenses

• Extend the core framework with additional guidance  
• AWS-provided lenses include:  
  – Serverless Lens  
  – SaaS Lens  
  – IoT Lens  
  – Machine Learning Lens  
• Create **custom lenses** to enforce internal standards  
• Adds additional questions and best practices to workload reviews  

---

## Integration with AWS Services

• **AWS Organizations** – Share workload reviews across multiple accounts  
• **AWS Service Catalog** – Enforce Well-Architected standards before deployment  
• **IAM** – Fine-grained access control for reviewing and editing workloads  

---

## Reporting and Tracking

• Retains historical review data for audits and compliance  
• Compare workloads over time to track risk reduction  
• Download reports in PDF format  
• Highlights risk trends and improvement progress  

---

## Security and Access Control

• Uses IAM for permissions and access control  
• Supports resource-level permissions  
• Data encrypted at rest and in transit  
• Fully managed, no infrastructure required  

---

## Pricing
